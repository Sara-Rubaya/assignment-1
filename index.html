Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Embrace innovation and technology to drive economic growth and foster a vibrant startup ecosystem.

Sustainability

Focus on sustainable development with green initiatives and eco-friendly infrastructure to address climate challenges.

Infrastructure

Strengthen infrastructure with efficient transport networks, smart cities, and improved connectivity to support growth.

Empowerment

Empower communities with inclusive growth, addressing regional and social inequalities, and uplifting marginalized groups
