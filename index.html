Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Embrace innovation and technology to drive economic growth and foster a vibrant startup ecosystem.

Sustainability

Focus on sustainable development with green initiatives and eco-friendly infrastructure to address climate challenges.

Infrastructure

Strengthen infrastructure with efficient transport networks, smart cities, and improved connectivity to support growth.

Empowerment

Empower communities with inclusive growth, addressing regional and social inequalities, and uplifting marginalized groups

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh 2.0 should focus on innovation, social entrepreneurship, and sustainability to address pressing challenges like poverty, unemployment, and environmental degradation. By empowering the youth, fostering inclusive growth, and embracing social business models, we can build a prosperous nation where no one is left behind, ensuring a fair and sustainable future for all."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
